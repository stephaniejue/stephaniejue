About Me

I’m a recent graduate from LEARN Academy, an immersive full-stack coding bootcamp. I’ve been interested in technology and computers since first exposed to my favorite math lab game, Maze Wars. My experience developed my ability to communicate with cross-functional teams and drive problem solving in a technical environment. After exploring front-end development on Free Code Camp, an online curriculum for web development, I knew this coding was my passion. My goal is to take on a more creative role at a fast-paced, team-driven company where I can help build web applications that provide smart, unique, and user-friendly experiences.
